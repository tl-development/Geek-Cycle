Donation on Demand

Geek Cycle is a Chicago community organization. We provide a marketplace where you can buy, sell or donate technology supplies and services from members of your local community.

How It Works

Let's say you bought a new computer but your old computer is useable and you want to sell or donate it to someone. Log onto Geek Cycle, upload a photo and description, and we will match you with a user in need. You can turn your unwanted tech supplies into cash or donations.

    • Who We Are

    Geek Cycle was founded in August 2017 by Lily MacFaydian as a way to provide free and low-cost technology resources to economically disadvantaged individuals and not-for-profit organizations. The vision of Geek Cycle is to create a community of technology professionals who are interested in supporting each other and ensure a clean environment for the next generation.

    Geek Cycle:

    • Is a community organization that offers a marketplace to buy, sell or donate technology supplies and services.
    • Recycles non-reusable materials in an ethical, safe, and environmentally responsible manner.
    • Works to create a strong participatory community by offering online tutorials and networking events.

    Lily MacFaydian - Founder

    A graduate of the School of the Art Institute Chicago, Lily is a freelance visual designer and front-end developer. Lily chose to focus her career on taking measurable steps to make a positive and meaningful impact on society by volunteering for non-profit organizations. In her free time, she enjoys geeking out with the latest technology, reading comics, playing video and board games, kayaking, cooking and yoga.
